What is Co-Make?

Co-Make is a hub created for local community members in order to identify, prioritize and solve problems in their area. The process is simple: you identify a problem, your community upvotes the issues they find important, and your local government or HOA solves it.

Features

Co-make sorts issues by zip code, allowing users to quickly find their area and get involved in seconds, whether to support an existing issue or add your own

An upvote system provides a fully democratic process to rank issues and begin problem-solving

Users can show appreciation for active community members by offering rewards, encouraging community engagement
